About

I’m an African - American multidisciplinary artist whose practice consists of narrative paintings of BIPOC and the LGBTQ+ community, digital drawings, community art projects, photography, and many more.

I am a first-generation college graduate who studied computer science and art, which led to my interests in the relationship between technology and art and other people’s experiences with it. I focus on giving a voice to a community or an important topic through my art and UI designs to help people.
